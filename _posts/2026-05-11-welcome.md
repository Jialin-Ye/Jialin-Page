This site is my public academic notebook. I plan to use it for three kinds of writing:

- **Notes** for research ideas, reading summaries, technical details, and working references.
- **Thoughts** for short reflections that do not need to become formal essays.
- **Travel journals** for places, conferences, field notes, and fragments worth remembering.

The structure is intentionally simple. I can write locally in VS Code, save each entry as a Markdown file in `_posts/`, and publish by pushing to GitHub.
