Hi, I am Jialin Ye. This website is my academic home on the web: a place for my profile, publications, research notes, thoughts, and travel journals.

I keep the site intentionally Markdown-first so that writing and maintenance stay lightweight. The main content lives in a few predictable places:

- Profile text: `_pages/about.md`
- Publications: `_bibliography/papers.bib`
- Notes, thoughts, and travel journals: `_posts/`
- CV data: `_data/cv.yml`
- Images and PDFs: `assets/img/` and `assets/pdf/`

The goal is simple: edit locally in VS Code, preview when needed, push to GitHub, and let GitHub Actions publish the updated site automatically.
